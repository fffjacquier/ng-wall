What is this about?

This is a personal training tool for me, both as walker, web programmer and on my own photographic journey

Goals of this site

My goals with this site are pretty straightforward:

  • • first and foremost, practice coding, let it be php, mysql, javascript, angularjs — or jquery
  • • so not only the front, but also the back
  • • learn angularjs by the way — and the angular way
  • • show pictures of paris in an unexpected way
  • • show an unacademic form of art, which is not a modern parietal art at all
  • • show hidden beauty of the world, things you passed in front of and never took a look
  • • write anything stupid or brilliant coming to my mind when looking at images
  • • meet various and different people
  • • change the world. Graffiti and graffiti people can change the world, and save people; it has already been done many times in the past; it can help people understand suffering. As Gandhi said, it's our suffering that will make them understand thier injustice. People writing their names because it's all they have or because they want to read it everywhere, or because they can't exist, or because they want to be part of something bigger than themselves, or because they want to be rich and famous and cannot afford to be on TV. Next step could be jail or terrorism or madness or simple life or nothing.
